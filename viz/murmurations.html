Murmurations of elliptic curves

Stage M1 — replicating He–Lee–Oliver–Pozdnyakov (arXiv:2204.10140), Figure 1. The mean of ap over rank-classed elliptic curves in a conductor window oscillates with p; even/odd rank oscillate in antiphase. ap is computed from scratch (good primes) and Atkin–Lehner-converted (bad primes); rank and conductor are oracle (Cremona ecdata).
