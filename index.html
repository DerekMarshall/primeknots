primeknots

Arithmetic topology and elliptic-curve murmurations, made executable — named theorems of number theory verified in from-scratch C++, with visualizations rendered from emitted JSON. "Verified" means numerically, over a stated range, twinned or oracle-refereed; no proofs are claimed.

Data note (research track, M5)

  • A pre-registered replication of the Sawin–Sutherland height-ordered murmuration — the headline write-up (PDF).
